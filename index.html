About the Venue

Amelia’s Resort is a private pool and function venue in Antipolo City—ideal for stress-free celebrations. Guests enjoy exclusive use of the entire resort, with an air-conditioned room and a fan room, covered dining area, videoke entertainment, and essential amenities for a comfortable stay.

  • ✅ 1 Air-conditioned room with 2 queen-size beds
  • ✅ 1 Fan room good for 5–6 pax
  • ✅ Use of Videoke
  • ✅ Use of Refrigerator
  • ✅ Use of Stove
  • ✅ 1 Free 5-gallon water container

Capacity: 30 guests (max 40). Excess charge applies; see rates below.

Amenities
